A little bit how I decided to learn coding

When I was 15 I'd decided to be a Graphic Designer, after waching my father working in some projects at home. I'd started my Design course at the university and in my last year I'd found my first job in my area. I worked as a designer for 15 years, studied more, learn English and improve my skills. The time was passing and the things became worse in my hometown, so I decided to move to another country and start everything again.
I choose Ireland as my new home, but to do it I had to step back in my career and accept any type of jobs to pay my courses, bills and also save money to renew my course and keep going to achieve my goals. The life here as a foreign student is really hard, but better than in my country.
I was studying English for the past 3 years, and I'm glad to say that now I'm in the second year of Computer Science, struggling a bit with some fields but proud of the results that I'd achieve.
If you are considering to change career, don't give up, keep going, study, practice and brief! üí™
